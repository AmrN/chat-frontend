Continuity & Recovery Assessments & Planning

Known by many names and acronyms such as Business Continuance Planning (BCP), Continuity of Operations Planning (COOP), Disaster Recovery Planning (DR, DRP) with each representing various activities which are often divergent across organizations . . . assuring your organization is protected from the unexpected continues to be of prime importance.

The Dauntless Team can rapidly conduct a comprehensive assessment of your preparation and prevention activities as well as your response plan, identify risk and suggest mitigation paths.

Technology Planning, Roadmaps and Assessments

How much will it cost? What's the ROI? Should we upgrade now? Which technology is a dead end and which will become the new industry standard? Is this technology worth the cost? What is the value proposition?

The Dauntless Team has extensive experience answering technology questions via feasibility studies, organization specific technology lifecycle process development, proof of concept and production pilot efforts, infrastructure service plans, as well as service management and organizational plans.

Data Center Planning, Design, Optimization

Data center managers are faced with challenges from every angle: today's data centers short on space, power and cooling. There is organizational pressure to go green, virtualize, and automate. And often to do it all within operational budgets.

The Dauntless Group can help. Our experienced consultants can help you design, plan, deploy and optimize your data center.

Information Security Compliance Planning

Securing information is not only good business sense, but is often required by industry and government regulations and laws.

The Dauntless Team can help you develop the procedures, processes, guidelines and standards necessary to assure your organizations continued compliance with a rapidly changing landscape of information security requirements.
